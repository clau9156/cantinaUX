Chicken Caesar Salad

  • Time: 20-30mins
  • Serves: 4
  • Rating: ☆★★★★

Description

A classic chicken salad recipe, featuring crunchy croutons and a creamy, garlic dressing. Ideal for lunch with friends.

Ingredients:
Thick slices crusty white bread 4
Olive oil 3tbsp
Salt (Sea salt)
Skinless, boneless chicken breasts 2
Large romaine lettuce 1
Caesar Dressing
Parmesan
Utensils:
Baking tray (oven)
Pan
Peeler or Grater
2 Knives
Cutting board
Bowl
Nutrition:
Calories 200
Fat 14(g) 18%
Protein 12(g) 24%
Sugar 1(g)
Fiber 1(g) 4%
Sodium 480(mg) 21%

Iron 0.7(mg) 4%

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas 6. Tear 1 medium ciabatta into big, ragged croutons or, if you prefer, cut with a bread knife. Spread over a large baking sheet or tray and sprinkle over 2 tbsp olive oil.
  2. Rub the oil into the bread and season with a little salt if you like (sea salt crystals are best for this). Bake for 8-10 mins, turning the croutons a few times during cooking so they brown evenly.
  3. Rub 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts with 1 tbsp olive oil, season. Place pan over a medium heat for 1 min, until hot, but not smoking. Lay the chicken on the pan (it will sizzle if it’s hot enough) and leave for 4 mins.
  1. Turn the chicken, then cook for 4 mins more. Check if it’s cooked by poking the tip of a sharp knife into the thickest part; there should be no sign of pink and juices will run clear.
  2. Shave/Grate the cheese with a peeler.
  3. Tear 1 large cos or romaine lettuce into large pieces and put in a large bowl.
  4. Pull chicken into bite-size strips and scatter half over the leaves, along with half the croutons.
  5. Add most of the dressing and toss with your fingers. Scatter the rest of the chicken and croutons, then drizzle with the remaining dressing. Sprinkle the parmesan on top and serve straight away.
